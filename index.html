Introduction

Nathaniel Paul Ferguson, also known as Nate, was born and raised in south Minneapolis, MN. While being involved with competitive team sports since age seven, he attended Clara Barton Open School from kindergarten until eighth grade. Nate then enrolled and graduated from Minneapolis Southwest High School. He played baseball all four years and by his senior year was named co-captain of the varsity team. Nate currently is working toward majoring in business administration focusing on management, and he also is a member of the Luther Baseball program. His interest in business comes from his opportunities as an intern, working for a corporation known as Meet Minneapolis. His biggest interests are in sports and also likes photograpgy all while exploring new things. His goals in life is to do everything with a purpose and be successful in all areas of life.
